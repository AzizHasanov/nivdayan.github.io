Niv Dayan

Postdoctoral Researcher
Harvard University

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Instutute for Applied Computational Sciences as well as the Data Systems Lab at Harvard University. My work is with Stratos Idreos and Pavlos Papa. My research interests are managing big data for scientific applications, especially astronomy. I am also interested in adaptive database indexing as well as flash memory.
